Account Deletion Policy

At TanEasy, we prioritize your privacy and provide a straightforward way to delete your account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account by following these steps:

  1. Open the TanEasy mobile app.
  2. Navigate to Settings > Account.
  3. Select Delete My Account and confirm your action.
  4. Alternatively, you may email us at info@taneasyapp.co.uk from your registered email address to request account deletion.

What Happens to Your Data?

Upon requesting account deletion, the following data will be removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, etc.)
  • Session history and app usage data
  • Stored preferences and linked device data

In compliance with our data retention policy, your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of the request, unless retention is required by law.

Need Assistance?

If you encounter any issues or have questions about your data or the deletion process, feel free to contact our support team at info@taneasyapp.co.uk.
