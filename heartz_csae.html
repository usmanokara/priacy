Reporting & Contact

If you need to report urgent suspected CSAE content or behavior, contact local law enforcement immediately. To notify Heartz directly, email our safety team from the email address associated with your account:

Email: heartzdating@gmail.com

When contacting us, include as much information as possible (usernames, timestamps, message IDs, screenshots) to help our trust & safety team review and act quickly.
