Account Deletion Policy

At KLIPZ APP, operated by Kaoz TV LLC, we respect your privacy and provide you with the ability to permanently delete your account and associated data from our Service.

How to Delete Your Account

You may request account deletion using one of the following methods:

  1. Open the KLIPZ mobile app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Select Delete My Account and confirm your request.
  4. Alternatively, email us from your registered email address at info@klipzapp.com with the subject line “Account Deletion Request”.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Once your deletion request is verified and confirmed, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account information (username, email address, profile details)
  • Your uploaded content (videos, audio, images, captions)
  • Your comments, messages, and interactions
  • Your preferences and account settings

Data Retention

We may retain certain information where required or permitted by law, including for safety, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or enforcement of our Terms of Service. Backup systems may take up to 30 days to fully purge deleted data.

Important Information

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.
  • If you log back into your account before deletion is finalized, your request may be canceled.
  • Public content previously shared by others (such as reposts or mentions) may remain visible.

Need Help?

If you experience any issues or have privacy-related questions, contact us at info@klipzapp.com.
