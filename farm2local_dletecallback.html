Farm2Local – Account Deletion Policy

At Farm2Local, operated by Two Helping Hands Inc., we respect your privacy and provide a clear and simple way to delete your account and associated personal data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion using one of the following methods:

  1. Open the Farm2Local mobile app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Select Delete My Account and confirm.
  4. Alternatively, email us from your registered email address at help@twohelpinhhands.com.au with the subject line “Account Deletion Request – Farm2Local”.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Once your request is confirmed, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile information (name, email, optional profile details)
  • Farm or product listings you created
  • Saved preferences and app usage data
  • Location data associated with your account

We do not store payment or credit card details. Any payments are handled by third-party providers such as Stripe and are governed by their own policies.

Deletion Timeline

Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request, unless retention is required by law or for legitimate compliance purposes.

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion or your privacy rights, please contact us at help@twohelpinhhands.com.au.
