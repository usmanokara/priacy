Account Deletion Policy

At NC Moto Lab, LLC, we respect your privacy and provide you with full control over your personal information. If you decide to delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems in accordance with our privacy policy.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account by following these steps:

  1. Open the NC Moto Lab mobile app.
  2. Navigate to Settings → Account.
  3. Select Delete My Account and confirm your decision.
  4. Alternatively, you can send an email request to info@ncmotolab.com using your registered email address.

What Happens After Deletion?

Once you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted from our records within 30 days unless retention is required by law:

  • Your personal details (name, email, etc.)
  • Any journey history or location data associated with your account
  • All stored preferences and app settings

After deletion, your information cannot be recovered. Please ensure that you have downloaded any data you wish to keep prior to requesting deletion.

Data Storage & Access

For NC Moto Lab Nav, all travel and journey data is accessible only to the end user and not stored locally on our servers. Users retain exclusive access to their travel information.

Need Assistance?

If you have any questions or encounter issues with your account, please contact our support team at info@ncmotolab.com.
