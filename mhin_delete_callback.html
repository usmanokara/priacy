Account Deletion Policy

At MHin, we value your privacy and make it simple to delete your account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account by following these steps:

  1. Open the MHin mobile app.
  2. Go to Settings > Account.
  3. Select Delete My Account and confirm your action.
  4. Alternatively, email us at info@fikragroup.uk from your registered email address to request account deletion.

What Happens to Your Data?

Once you request account deletion, the following will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, etc.)
  • Your service listings and associated media
  • Any stored preferences and app usage data

All personal data will be deleted within 30 days of your request unless retention is required by law.

Need Assistance?

If you have any questions or issues regarding your account or data, contact our support team at info@fikragroup.uk.
