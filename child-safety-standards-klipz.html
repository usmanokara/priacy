Klipz Child Safety Standards

At Klipz, the safety of children using our app is our top priority. We strictly prohibit and actively prevent any content involving child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

1. Content Monitoring and Removal

  • All content uploaded to Klipz is monitored using automated detection tools and human review to identify inappropriate material.
  • Any content suspected of containing CSAM is immediately removed.
  • Repeat offenders are banned from the platform.

2. User Reporting

  • Users can report child safety concerns directly within the app.
  • Reports are reviewed promptly by our trained moderation team.
  • We provide clear instructions in-app on how to report concerning content.

3. Staff Training

  • All Klipz moderators and relevant staff receive training on identifying CSAM and responding appropriately.
  • Staff follow strict guidelines to ensure user reports are handled safely and confidentially.

4. Compliance with Laws

  • Klipz complies with all applicable child protection laws and regulations in regions where our app is available.
  • Suspected CSAM is reported to the relevant regional and national authorities.
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement in investigations related to child safety.
