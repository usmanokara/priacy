Account Deletion Policy

At Heartz, we respect your privacy and make it simple to permanently delete your account and all associated data from our platform.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request to delete your Heartz account using one of the following methods:

  1. Open the Heartz app.
  2. Navigate to Settings → Account.
  3. Select Delete My Account and confirm your action.
  4. Alternatively, you may send an email from your registered address to heartzdating@gmail.com with the subject line “Account Deletion Request”.

What Happens After Deletion?

Once your request is confirmed, the following information will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your personal information (name, email address, date of birth, etc.)
  • Your photos, matches, messages, and connections
  • Your preferences and account activity history

All data will be deleted within 30 days of your confirmed request, unless retention is legally required for fraud prevention or compliance purposes.

Important Notes

  • Once deleted, your account and data cannot be recovered.
  • If you log in again within 30 days of requesting deletion, your request will be canceled.
  • Transaction or legal records (if any) may be retained as required by law.

Need Assistance?

If you face any issues deleting your account or wish to inquire about data handling, contact our support team at heartzdating@gmail.com.
