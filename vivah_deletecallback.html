Vivah – Account Deletion Request

At Vivah, we respect your privacy and provide a clear way for users to delete their account and associated personal data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Vivah account using the following methods:

  1. Open the Vivah mobile app.
  2. Navigate to Profile → Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your request.
  5. Alternatively, you can send an email request from your registered email address to hello.vivah@gmail.com with the subject line “Account Deletion Request – Vivah”.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, age, gender, photos, and bio)
  • Dating preferences and match data
  • Messages sent and received within the app
  • Account settings and preferences
  • Associated device and usage data

Please note that we do not store credit card or payment details. All payments and subscriptions are processed by secure third-party providers such as Apple In-App Purchase or Google Play Billing.

Data Retention

After receiving your request, your account and personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Some limited information may be retained if required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or security purposes.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about deleting your account or your privacy rights, please contact us at hello.vivah@gmail.com.
