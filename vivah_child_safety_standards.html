Vivah – Child Safety Standards

At Vivah, we are committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have a strict zero-tolerance policy against any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

These standards outline how Vivah prevents, detects, and responds to content or behavior that may exploit, harm, or endanger minors.

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

Vivah strictly prohibits any content, communication, or activity that involves Child Sexual Abuse or Exploitation (CSAE). This includes but is not limited to:

  • Sharing or distributing child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexualized images, videos, or content involving minors
  • Grooming behavior intended to exploit or manipulate minors
  • Soliciting sexual content from individuals under 18
  • Attempting to arrange meetings with minors for sexual purposes
  • Any activity that exploits, harms, or endangers a child

2. Age Restrictions

Vivah is intended only for individuals 18 years of age or older. Users who attempt to misrepresent their age or create accounts as minors will have their accounts suspended or permanently banned.

3. Content Monitoring & Enforcement

To protect users and prevent abuse, Vivah may implement a combination of automated moderation tools and manual review processes to detect harmful or inappropriate content.

If CSAE content or behavior is detected, we will take immediate action, including:

  • Immediate removal of the violating content
  • Suspension or permanent termination of the user account
  • Reporting confirmed violations to relevant law enforcement authorities
  • Cooperation with investigations as required by law

4. Reporting Child Safety Concerns

Users can report suspicious or harmful behavior directly through the app's reporting features or by contacting our safety team.

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, please contact your local law enforcement authorities immediately.

You may also report concerns to our safety team at:

Email: hello.vivah@gmail.com

5. Cooperation with Authorities

Vivah fully cooperates with law enforcement and child protection organizations when investigating reports related to child safety, exploitation, or abuse.

6. Continuous Improvement

We continuously review and improve our safety policies, moderation systems, and reporting mechanisms to better protect users and prevent abuse on our platform.
