Vivah Vibe – Child Safety Standards

Vivah Vibe is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful community. We strictly prohibit any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and enforce strong policies to protect minors and vulnerable individuals.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Vivah Vibe has a zero-tolerance policy for any content, behavior, or communication that involves the sexual exploitation or abuse of minors. Any such activity will result in immediate removal of content and permanent account suspension.

2. Age Restrictions

Vivah Vibe is intended for adults only. Users must be at least 18 years old to create an account or use the platform.

3. Monitoring and Moderation

We actively monitor reports and user activity to detect and prevent harmful behavior. Content that violates our safety standards is removed promptly.

4. Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report suspicious profiles, messages, or content directly within the app. All reports are reviewed by our moderation team and appropriate action is taken.

5. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

Vivah Vibe cooperates fully with law enforcement authorities in investigating any reports related to child exploitation or illegal activities.

6. Contact for Safety Concerns

If you encounter any content or behavior that may involve the exploitation of minors, please contact us immediately:

Email: safety@vivahvibe.com

We are committed to creating a safe, respectful, and trusted environment for all users.
